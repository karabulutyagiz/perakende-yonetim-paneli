⚠️ PLEASE USE THE DEMO ACCOUNT BELOW — do NOT register a new

====================================================
HOW TO TEST
====================================================
1. Launch the app. Login screen shows "ParaSende" with logo.
2. Sign in with the demo account in the fields below.
3. Products screen opens. Top bar icons: Gelen Siparişler
   (orders), Faturalar (invoices), Raporlar (reports),
   Borçlar (debts), Hesabım (account).
4. Add items to cart (right panel on iPad / FAB on iPhone).
   Tap "Faturayı oluştur" to open invoice flow, pick a customer
   (or "Yeni müşteri ekle" to create one in-app), pick payment,
   send the invoice. The invoice appears under Faturalar.
5. Tap the receipt icon on any invoice → PDF preview, savable
   to Photos.
6. Reports tab shows daily/weekly/monthly sales charts.

====================================================
IN-APP ACCOUNT DELETION (Guideline 5.1.1(v))
====================================================
1. From the Products screen, tap "Hesabım" (Account) icon top
   right (person silhouette).
2. Scroll to the red "Tehlikeli bölge" (Danger zone) section.
3. Tap "Hesabımı sil" (Delete my account).
4. Confirmation dialog asks for password and typed "SİL"
   (Turkish for DELETE).
5. Submitting calls DELETE /auth/account → permanently deletes
   the user account, the tenant business, all customers,
   products, orders, invoices, debts and payments. The user is
   logged out and routed back to the login screen.

Web fallback: https://toptanperakende.online/delete-account
