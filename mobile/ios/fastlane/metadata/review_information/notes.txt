ParaSende is a B2B wholesale order-management app for Turkish retail
business owners (wholesalers and small shops). The app is in Turkish.
No in-app purchases, no ads, no third-party tracking SDKs.

⚠️ PLEASE USE THE DEMO ACCOUNT BELOW TO TEST THE APP — do NOT register a
new account from the sign-up screen. New sign-ups go through a quick
manual admin approval (typically same business day), so a brand-new
account cannot log in immediately. The demo account is already approved
and has full functionality.

HOW TO TEST
1. Launch the app. The login screen shows "ParaSende" with our logo.
2. Sign in with the demo account below — a wholesale business owner
   (tenant_owner) account on our production backend, already approved:
   Email:    playreview@toptanpanel.com

3. After login you reach the Products screen. The top bar has icons
   for: Gelen Siparişler (incoming orders), Faturalar (invoices),
   Raporlar (reports), Borçlar (debts), Hesabım (account).
4. Add items to the cart (right-side cart panel on iPad / floating
   cart FAB on iPhone). Tap "Faturayı oluştur" to open the invoice
   flow, pick a customer (or tap "Yeni müşteri ekle" to create one
   in-app), pick payment method, send the invoice. The invoice
   appears under Faturalar.
5. Tap the receipt icon on any invoice to view a PDF that can be
   saved to Photos.
6. Reports tab shows daily/weekly/monthly sales charts.

HOW TO TEST IN-APP ACCOUNT DELETION (Guideline 5.1.1(v))
1. From the Products screen, tap the "Hesabım" (Account) icon at the
   top right (person silhouette).
2. Scroll to the red "Tehlikeli bölge" (Danger zone) section.
3. Tap "Hesabımı sil" (Delete my account).
4. A confirmation dialog asks for the account password and the word
   "SİL" (Turkish for DELETE) as a typed confirmation.
5. Submitting calls DELETE /auth/account on the backend, which
   permanently deletes the user account, the tenant business, all
   customers, products, orders, invoices, debts and payments. The
   user is logged out and routed back to the login screen.

A web fallback for account deletion is also available at
https://toptanperakende.online/legal/delete-account.html

The app talks to our production backend over HTTPS only
(https://toptanperakende.online). Support: destek@toptanperakende.online
